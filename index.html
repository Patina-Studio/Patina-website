The Artist Renoir

French Artists: Renoir

Pierre‑Auguste Renoir was a French artist and a major figure in the Impressionist movement.
Born in 1841, he was known for his use of light, warm colours, and scenes of everyday life.
Renoir often focused on people, showing social moments in a lively and natural way.

Key facts about Renoir:

  • French Impressionist painter
  • Painted scenes from daily life
  • Focused strongly on people

My favourite Painting -The Umbrellas:

  1. Figures appear mid movement in a busy street
  2. Different umbrella angles suggest movement
  3. Clothing and facial details add realism
